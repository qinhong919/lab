摘要 / Abstract

国内外超心理学研究由来已久。本文探讨现代中外超心理学研究的某些特点，并比较中外研究方法和领域的异同。

The study of parapsychology has a long history. This paper compares the similarities and differences on methods and fields between Chinese and foreign research.
